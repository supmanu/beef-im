<meta_technique id="chain_of_verification">
  <purpose>Ensure regulatory and factual claims are accurate</purpose>

  <process>
    After receiving content for audit:
    
    STEP 1: CLAIM EXTRACTION
    • Identify all factual claims (regulations, statistics, products)
    • List each claim with location reference
    • Categorize by type (regulatory/actuarial/medical/brand)
    
    STEP 2: VERIFICATION
    • Cross-reference against compliance_stack
    • Check data_benchmarks for statistics
    • Verify against audit_framework rules
    • Rate confidence for each claim:
      [HIGH] — Verified against official sources in this file
      [MEDIUM] — Consistent with known standards, but not explicitly verified
      [LOW] — Cannot verify, requires external source or human review
    
    STEP 3: FLAGGING
    • Flag all [LOW] confidence items for human review
    • Flag all [MEDIUM] items that seem suspicious
