**The Bottom Line:**

This plan gives you the speed of private healthcare without the bankruptcy risk of the American system—and without the wait times of the Canadian system.

## Understanding Your Coverage: Two Types of Benefits

This portfolio provides two distinct types of benefits. Understanding the difference is crucial.

### Type 1: Medical Bills Covered (Paid to the Hospital)

**AIA Health Happy 25M** pays your medical bills directly to the hospital. You walk in, receive treatment, and walk out without paying out of pocket (within coverage limits).

- Annual coverage: 25,000,000 THB
- Doubles to 50,000,000 THB annually for 4 years if diagnosed with critical illness
- Hospital room: 9,000 THB per day (actual cost for ICU)
- OPD (Outpatient): 2,000 THB per visit per day (up to 30 visits per year)

**This covers the bills. But what about your income? Your mortgage? Your family's living expenses while you recover?**

That's where Type 2 comes in.

---

### Type 2: Cash Compensation (Paid Directly to You)

These riders pay **cash directly into your bank account**—not to the hospital. You decide how to use this money: cover lost income, pay for a caregiver, fly family members to Thailand, or simply have financial breathing room during recovery.

**💸 HB Extra 10,000 (Cash Benefit)**

| Trigger | Payout |
|---------|--------|
| Daily hospital stay | 10,000 THB per day |
| ICU admission | 30,000 THB per day |
| Surgery with anesthesia | 50,000 THB per procedure |
| Acute critical illness hospitalization (13 diseases) | 250,000 THB per occurrence |

**Example:** You're hospitalized for 10 days including 3 days in ICU and require one surgery.
- Regular days: 7 × 10,000 = 70,000 THB
- ICU days: 3 × 30,000 = 90,000 THB
- Surgery: 50,000 THB
- **Total cash to you: 210,000 THB**

This money is yours. The hospital bills are handled separately by Health Happy.

**🤞 Care for Cancer (Cash Benefit)**

| Trigger | Payout |

**Example:** Diagnosed with Stage 3 cancer.
- Multi-Pay CI pays: 5,000,000 THB (or 2,000,000 THB for Option C)
- CI Procare pays: 3,000,000 THB (Option C only)
- **Total immediate cash: up to 8,000,000 THB**

This is on top of your medical bills being covered and your daily cash benefits from Care for Cancer.

---

**🛡️ CI Procare (Lump Sum + Premium Waiver)**

CI Procare adds 3,000,000 THB in critical illness coverage with a unique benefit: **the premium does not go to waste**.

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Sum insured | 3,000,000 THB |
| Premium payment period | Fixed for 20 years |
| Coverage period | Until age 99 |
| Premium waiver | If you claim, all future premiums are waived for life |

**Premium Waiver Triggers (Same as Multi-Pay CI Plus):**

Your CI Procare premium is waived for life if **either** of the following occurs:

**Trigger 1 — Severe Critical Illness Diagnosis:**
If you are diagnosed with a covered severe critical illness (cancer, heart attack, stroke, kidney failure, etc.), you receive the full sum insured as a lump sum payment AND all future premiums are waived for life.

**Trigger 2 — Major Impact Benefit (MIB):**
If any of the following occurs with 5+ consecutive days in ICU:

1. **Major Surgery** requiring general or regional anesthesia (excluding organ transplant) with 5+ consecutive days in ICU
2. **Respiratory support** via intubation or ventilator with 5+ consecutive days in ICU
3. **Major organ failure** at the final stage, treated according to modern medical standards, with 5+ consecutive days in ICU

Once triggered, you receive your full sum insured AND never pay another premium for this coverage.

**Why Option C?**

**The Rent vs. Mortgage Principle**

Think of Option A as **Rent** — you pay every year, and at the end, you own nothing. If you stop paying, coverage stops.

Think of Option C as a **Mortgage** — you pay more upfront, but you're building equity. After 20 years, you own the coverage outright. And if you decide to "sell" (surrender), you get your money back.

The monthly payment is higher, but the net cost to you after 20 years is virtually zero.

**The "Premium Does Not Go to Waste" Principle:**

| Scenario | What Happens |
|----------|--------------|
| **You stay healthy for 20 years** | At age 50, you can surrender CI Procare and receive cash value approximately equal to your total premiums paid. You've essentially had free critical illness coverage for 20 years. |
| **You get diagnosed with critical illness** | You receive 3,000,000 THB immediately. All future CI Procare premiums are waived for life. Coverage continues until age 99. If the condition recurs, you can claim again (up to 600% maximum). |
| **You pass away or live to age 99** | Your beneficiaries (or you) receive the full sum insured or the surrender value, whichever is higher. |

**After 20 Years (Age 50):**

Once CI Procare is fully paid, you only pay for the health riders (Health Happy, HB Extra, Care for Cancer, Multi-Pay CI). Your critical illness coverage from CI Procare continues at zero additional cost for life.

**The Long-Term Savings:**

If you maintained traditional critical illness coverage until age 99, you would pay significantly more—potentially over 10,000,000 THB more—than the CI Procare route. Starting at age 30 gives you the maximum value for your money.

## Why Health Happy 25M?

**1. Sufficient Coverage for Current Medical Costs**

Health Happy 25M provides an annual coverage limit of 25,000,000 THB. This is more than sufficient for virtually any medical treatment in Thailand, including complex surgeries, extended ICU stays, and cancer treatment.

The coverage resets to 25,000,000 THB every policy year, ensuring continuous protection.

**2. Enhanced Coverage When You Need It Most**

If you are diagnosed with a critical illness such as cancer, your annual coverage **doubles to 50,000,000 THB** for that year—and remains at 50,000,000 THB annually for the next 3 years (4 years total). After this period, coverage returns to the standard 25,000,000 THB per year and continues for life.

This provides a buffer against medical inflation and the high costs associated with ongoing critical illness treatment during the most intensive treatment years.

**3. Flexibility to Adjust as You Age**

This is the most important consideration for long-term planning.

As you get older, health insurance premiums increase significantly. At age 70, 80, or beyond, annual premiums may become substantial. At that stage, you may decide that 25M in coverage is more than you need.

**Health Happy 25M allows you to downgrade:**

| Current Plan | Downgrade Options |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Health Happy 25M | → 15M → 5M → 1M |

You can reduce your coverage—and your premium—without canceling the policy or losing your insurability. This is critical because if you cancel and try to purchase a new policy at age 75, you will likely be declined due to age or health conditions.

**This flexibility is your long-term safety valve.** You lock in insurability now, at age 30, and retain the option to adjust later.
