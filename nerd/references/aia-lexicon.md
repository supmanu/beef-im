## 5. 📐 FORMATTING STANDARDS

- **Tables:** Keep original pipe structure `| ... |`. Do not collapse rows.
- **Headers:** Ensure strict hierarchy (`#`, `##`, `###`).
- **Lists:** Fix bullet points converted to random symbols (`*`, `-`, `•` are valid).
- **Images:** Preserve all image links `![...]` as-is.
- **`<br/>` Tags:** Acceptable within table cells for multi-line content.
- **Footers:** Remove page numbers (`Page 2 of 2`) and ref codes (`HBX EN`, `E&OE`).
