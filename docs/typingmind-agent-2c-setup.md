*   **API Host:** `https://api.minimax.chat/v1` (Standard) or `https://api.minimax.io/v1` (International)

*   **Model ID:** `abab6.5s-chat` (This is the identifier for **Minimax 01 (M2.1)**).

*   **Max Tokens:** `4096` or `8192`.
