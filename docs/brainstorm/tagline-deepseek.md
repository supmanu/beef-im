## Three Favorites (DeepSeek's pick)

**#9** lands the Paradox-first register best — it inverts the cheap-=safe assumption without preaching. "แพงไม่จำเป็น" is how Mass Affluent Thai actually talk, and the ending "สำคัญ" pivots the energy cleanly. Works as both a /manifesto header and an article pull quote.

**#3** has the double-barrel rhythm that sticks. The parallel "ไม่มีน้ำในสัญญา ไม่มีน้ำในจาน" is a one-liner you can leave as a footer quote indefinitely — it captures the whole brand paradox in nine words without any English crutch.

**#13** wins the Direct register for social bio. "กินเนื้อ วิเคราะห์สัญญา" is five words, one breath, both halves doing equal work. It's the most tweetable/stickerable of the batch and works natively in a LINE OA bio.
