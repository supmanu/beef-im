beef.im — UI/UX Direction Preview 5 · Cinematic Hero × Editorial Body Hybrids

Fifth preview, addressing the gap none of the 14 LLM proposals filled: the legacy Nerd-with-Nart pattern of cinematic hero + editorial body. Every model in the original brainstorm interpreted "เน้นเนื้อๆ ไม่เอาน้ำ" as "no theatrical hero" — they all converged on uniform restraint. But the brand thesis ประกันเนื้อๆ deserves the contradiction enacted: a showoff hero (the contradiction made cinematic) followed by a substance body (the contradiction delivered).

Each mockup below shows BOTH layers in one frame — the cinematic hero on top, the editorial body beneath, with a subtle transition rule between them. Scroll past the mockup hero in your imagination; that's where the editorial register takes over.

Pattern thesis. Hero and body don't have to share visual register. Legacy NWN proved this — EmberGlow particles + atmospheric mountain depth on the homepage, then editorial reading register everywhere else. The cinematic moment earns the editorial substance that follows. None of Picks 1–5 from Preview1 break — they just gain a detachable hero module.

Hero spec (the brand-specific part)

All five cinematic techniques from across the bake-off, combined into ONE custom hero module:

② Slow conic-gradient sunburst top-right (rotating, 32s) — from Provenance

Pure CSS, ~3 KB, zero image assets, GPU-composited, prefers-reduced-motion compliant. The brand mark is the hero — no photography required.

Body — your choice

This hero is body-agnostic. The example below uses Investment Grade (light cream + rating badges in 2-col grid), but the hero works equally for:
— Pick 1 Investment Grade · Pick 2 Docket# · Pick 3 Weekend Dossier · Pick 4 Temperature Grade · Pick 5 Engraved · Swing alt Open Market
Decide the editorial register based on voice (institutional vs. vernacular) without recompiling the hero.

Why this is the most strategically flexible

The other three hybrids couple a specific hero to a specific body. This one decouples them. Build the signature hero once — it becomes the brand's recognizable opening regardless of which editorial direction wins for the body. If you change your mind on the body register six months from now, the hero stays. If you A/B test two body registers (Investment Grade vs. Open Market), the hero stays. The cinematic moment becomes infrastructure, not a styling choice.

Direct lineage to legacy

This is the strongest evolution path from the legacy Nerd-with-Nart pattern. NWN had EmberGlow + Snowstorm as detachable atmospheric components. Hybrid D extends the principle: the entire hero becomes a detachable component, designed once and applied across whichever body direction ships. The brand thesis lives in the hero (ดูเนื้อ ไม่ดูหน้า + atmospheric depth + temperature teaser), the brand's content discipline lives in the body. Legacy code from EmberGlow.tsx at 3c8b871 drops directly into this hero unchanged.
