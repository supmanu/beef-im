Why ship

Rating badge is the cleanest dual-content unification in the bake-off. Same 72×24px IBM Plex Mono component carries UNDERWEIGHT for whole life and WELL MARBLED for ribeye. The brand thesis becomes self-evident — every piece gets a verdict.

Component that bridges insurance + meat

The rating badge. Vocabulary tier: OVERWEIGHT · NEUTRAL · UNDERWEIGHT for insurance; WELL MARBLED · LEAN · AVOID for cuts. Same colors (navy / amber / red), same position, same rigor.

Trade-off

"The visual system protects nothing — it amplifies whatever the writing is. If the writing is hot, it becomes a weapon. If the writing is safe, it becomes a liability brochure." — Sonnet 4.6's own honest flag. Format risk: defaults to KBank wealth-management feel if voice slips into formal advisory register.

Why ship

Most durable system for a 5-year horizon. Light bg = mobile sunlight readable by default. Annotation depth instead of motion = won't trend-date. Carries investigative teardown, explainer, opinion, interview, and recipe in one editorial language without two-brand syndrome.

Component that bridges insurance + meat

The masthead block (desk tag · word count · read time) above every headline. Treats both content types as filed stories. The desk tag (POLICY DESK / FOOD DESK) frames the editorial seriousness without hiding either.

Trade-off

"Less instantly theatrical than Heat Haze and less aggressively ownable than Clause Block. You accept that trade in exchange for a system that can still feel premium five years from now, not just bold at launch." — GPT-5.4's own honest flag.

Why ship

Most genuinely novel idea across all 13 outputs. The temperature bar is literally the same chart for "insurance risk level" and "meat doneness level" — not a metaphor, a shared information-design language. You can scan the homepage and "read" the temperature of every article before clicking.

Component that bridges insurance + meat

The temperature bar (4px tall, gradient blue → amber → red) at the top of every card. Same gradient, semantic axis swaps:

Insurance → ความเสี่ยง: ต่ำ · ปานกลาง · สูง
Recipe → ความสุก: Rare · Medium · Well-done

Trade-off

Requires editorial discipline — every article gets a temperature value. Color must be tested for color-blind accessibility (blue → amber → red compresses for deuteranopia). Higher upfront design system cost than Picks 1–3.

Why ship

The only direction that owns Thai cultural depth as a design system, not as decoration. References the Ramkhamhaeng Stele directly. Carved-stone tactility maps ดูเนื้อ ไม่ดูหน้า as what's carved lasts; what's painted fades.

Component that bridges insurance + meat

The section number + carved data row. Both insurance metrics (COI, premium, illustrated value) and recipe metrics (core temp, rest time) get the same chiseled-stat treatment with gold-leaf values. Information presented as inscribed wisdom.

Trade-off

"The inset/chiseled visual language is harder to execute consistently across browsers — box-shadow inset stacking behaves differently on Safari vs. Chrome at sub-pixel widths. Demands disciplined QA.
