Signature

One-directional inner shadows — cards carved INTO the surface, not floating above. Gold-leaf section numbers. Chiseled headline reveal: text gradient sweep revealing carved depth on scroll entry. Sarabun ExtraBold + Fraunces at -0.03em tracking. The deepest Thai cultural ownership.
