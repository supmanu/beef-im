ไม่ผ่าน

Signature

Cold open: 90vh hero still pins in place while headline rises 24px and byline fades over 80vh of scroll. Content hidden until cinematic title card releases. Only motion on the entire page. After release: pure stillness.

Trade-off

Dark background = unreadable in Bangkok sunlight. Theatrical scroll-lock animation risks feeling like a "loading screen" on slow connections. Best for desktop and evening mobile reading. Unforgettable when it works — frustrating when it doesn't.
