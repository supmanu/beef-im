## Three Favorites (MiniMax M2.7's pick)

**#9 "จริงจังเรื่องสัญญา สบายเรื่องสเต็ก"** — The brand paradox in its simplest form. Four words, two halves, no pun needed. Works as manifesto header, LINE OA bio, or pull quote. The tension does all the work.

**#3 "ตัวเลขไม่หลอกลวง เนื้อไม่ต้องปิดบัง"** — Cleanest analytical line. Double negative construction (ไม่...ไม่...) is natural Thai cadence. "ปิดบัง" on the meat side carries both the steak-sear meaning and the "no BS" meaning simultaneously. One-breath footer.

**#13 "กินเนื้อ ดูสัญญา"** — Best pure-Direct one-liner. Four words, two verbs, both halves equal weight. The shortest complete expression of the brand. Sticker-ready for LINE stickers or Twitter bio.
